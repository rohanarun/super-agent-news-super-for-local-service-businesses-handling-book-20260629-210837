How Super compares across the agent landscape

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a strong general assistant for writing, research, and ad‑hoc help. It’s evolving toward agents, but it’s still best for conversational tasks rather than durable, repeatable computer workflows.

Gemini

Gemini is pushing computer control aggressively — including agentic booking and native computer use. It’s powerful, but designed as a broad platform layer rather than a focused, cost‑efficient operator for your specific workflows.

Siri

Siri remains voice‑first and tightly integrated with Apple devices. It’s convenient, but limited for multi‑step booking, quoting, and back‑office operations.

Grok

Grok emphasizes real‑time and social context. It’s opinionated and conversational, not optimized for running your scheduling or quoting software all day.

Folk & Orchids

Folk and Orchids represent niche and experimental approaches in the broader agent market. They highlight interest in automation, but aren’t purpose‑built for repeated computer‑use workflows in local services.
